Porsche SE is a holding company with investments in the areas of mobility and industrial technology. The company employs just under 50 people as of 31 December 2024. As core investments, Porsche SE holds the majority of the ordinary shares in Volkswagen AG and 25% plus one share of the ordinary shares in Porsche AG. In addition, Porsche SE acquired minority shareholdings in several technology companies in North America, Europe and Israel and invested in private equity and venture capital funds.
